The Moment

Wiedenbach Brown has already built something rare. A platform, a reputation, and a way of operating that others have spent decades trying to replicate.

They pioneered national account lighting in 1960. No one else saw it yet. That instinct to move first — to recognize where the market was going before it could name it — that's the DNA of this organization.

110 years later, the move hasn't changed. The channel is shifting. The intelligence behind every deal, every spec, every relationship now needs to operate differently — connected, visible, and scalable.

This is bigger than WB. This is a defining moment for USESI. Fourteen companies at an inflection point — where the next move determines who leads and who gets left behind.

The momentum is already here.

The only question is — are you ready to build it, or be built by it?
